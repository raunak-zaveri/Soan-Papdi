Soan Papdi

The Flaky Indian Sweet

What is Soan Papdi?

Soan Papdi is a popular Indian dessert known for its flaky, melt-in-the-mouth texture. It is often enjoyed during festivals, gifted during celebrations, or simply relished as a sweet treat after meals.

Ingredients

The main ingredients include gram flour, sugar, ghee, milk, and cardamom. Some variations add nuts like pistachios and almonds for extra crunch and flavor.
